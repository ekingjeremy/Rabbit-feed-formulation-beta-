## DISEASE MANAGEMENT

### Common Poultry Diseases in Nigeria:

1. **Newcastle Disease**
   - Highly contagious viral disease
   - Vaccination: Day 1, 3 weeks, 8 weeks, every 3 months

2. **Gumboro (Infectious Bursal Disease)**
   - Affects immune system
   - Vaccination: 10-14 days, booster at 21-28 days

3. **Fowl Pox**
   - Viral disease, mosquito-transmitted
   - Vaccination: 6-8 weeks

4. **Coccidiosis**
   - Intestinal parasitic disease
   - Prevention: Coccidiostats in feed, hygiene

5. **Chronic Respiratory Disease (CRD)**
   - Common in layers
   - Prevention: Good ventilation, reduce stress

## RECOMMENDED PRACTICES FOR NIGERIAN CLIMATE

### Housing:
- Open-sided with good cross-ventilation
- North-South orientation
- High ceilings (3-4 meters)
- Deep litter or cage systems
- Insulated roofing

### Heat Stress Management:
- Adequate ventilation (natural + fans)
- Provide cool water
- Feed during cooler hours
- Use heat-reducing supplements
- Reduce stocking density

### Biosecurity:
- Footbath at entrance
- Limit visitors
- All-in-all-out system
- Proper disposal of dead birds
- Regular disinfection

### Water Management:
- **Broilers:** 2-2.5 × feed intake
- **Layers:** 200-250 ml/bird/day
- Always fresh and clean
- Add vitamins during stress
