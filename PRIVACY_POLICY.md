# Privacy Policy for Necstech Feed Optimizer

**Last Updated: February 8, 2026**

## Introduction

Necstech ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Necstech Feed Optimizer mobile and web application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When using our Service, you may provide:

- **Animal Parameters**: Age, weight, feed intake requirements
- **Feed Formulations**: Ingredient combinations you create
- **Account Information**: Email address (if you choose to create an account)
- **Feedback**: Survey responses, support requests

### Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Performance Data**: Crash reports, error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide the Service**: Calculate feed formulations, predictions, and cost analysis
2. **Improve the Service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
3. **Fix Issues**: Debug errors and improve performance
4. **Communicate**: Send updates, respond to support requests (with your consent)
5. **Research**: Develop better AI models (using anonymized data only)

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All formulation data is stored **locally on your device**
- We do NOT upload your farm data to our servers
- You control your data - delete the app to remove all data

### Cloud Storage (Optional - Coming Soon)
- Optional cloud sync feature (not yet available)
- When available, data will be encrypted in transit and at rest
- You can opt-out at any time

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

1. **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting, analytics (Google Analytics)
2. **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect rights
3. **Business Transfers**: In case of merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

### Third-Party Services

Our Service may use:

- **Google Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics
- **Streamlit Cloud**: Hosting infrastructure (see Streamlit's privacy policy)
- **App Stores**: Google Play Store, Apple App Store

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
- Secure cloud infrastructure
- Regular security updates
- Limited employee access to data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your data
2. **Correction**: Update incorrect information
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
4. **Opt-Out**: Refuse optional data collection
